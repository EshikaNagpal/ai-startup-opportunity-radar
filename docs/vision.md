# Vision

## Product Name

AI Startup Opportunity Radar

## Problem

Every day, millions of people discuss frustrations, unmet needs, inefficient workflows, and product complaints across forums, reviews, communities, and social platforms.

These discussions contain valuable signals about potential startup opportunities. However, the volume of information is too large for any founder to analyze manually.

As a result, founders often rely on intuition, trends, or luck when deciding what to build, causing them to miss high-potential opportunities hidden in plain sight.

---

## Solution

AI Startup Opportunity Radar is an AI-powered market intelligence platform that continuously analyzes online discussions and identifies:

* Recurring user pain points
* Emerging market trends
* Underserved customer needs
* Startup opportunities backed by evidence

Instead of guessing what to build, founders receive data-driven insights about where demand is growing and where existing solutions are failing.

---

## Primary User

Early-stage founders and builders who are searching for startup ideas, market opportunities, and validated problems worth solving.

---

## Desired Outcome

Users should be able to answer the following questions within minutes:

* What problems are people discussing most frequently?
* Which markets are growing fastest?
* What complaints are increasing over time?
* Which opportunities have strong demand but weak competition?
* What should I build next?

---

## Long-Term Vision

Become the default intelligence platform for discovering startup opportunities.

Just as investors use Bloomberg terminals to understand financial markets, founders should use AI Startup Opportunity Radar to understand problem markets.

The platform should continuously monitor discussions, reviews, communities, product launches, and market signals across the internet and transform them into actionable startup opportunities.

A founder should never have to wonder what to build next. The platform should surface opportunities with supporting evidence, market signals, competitive analysis, and clear recommendations.

---

### Mid-Term

### Long-Term
