# User Persona

## Primary User

Early-Stage Founder

---

## Profile

A founder, builder, indie hacker, or aspiring entrepreneur who is actively looking for startup opportunities and market gaps.

Typically:

* Age: 20-40
* Technical or semi-technical
* Interested in building products
* Limited resources and time
* Wants evidence before committing to an idea

---

## Goals

* Discover startup opportunities before competitors
* Identify real customer pain points
* Avoid building products nobody wants
* Validate ideas faster
* Find underserved markets

---

## Current Process

Today they:

* Browse Reddit
* Read Hacker News
* Explore Product Hunt
* Follow startup content on X/Twitter
* Read forums and reviews
* Talk to potential users

The process is slow, manual, and inconsistent.

---

## Frustrations

* Information overload
* Too many ideas, too little validation
* Hard to identify patterns across discussions
* Difficult to know which opportunities are real
* Fear of building something nobody wants

---

## Desired Outcome

Instead of spending weeks researching, they want:

* A ranked list of opportunities
* Evidence supporting each opportunity
* Trend data
* Competition analysis
* Clear recommendations

---

## Success Scenario

A founder opens the platform and sees:

1. Top emerging opportunities
2. User complaints driving demand
3. Growth trends
4. Competition level
5. Suggested startup concepts

The founder can quickly decide what to explore next.

---

## Why They Would Use Our Product

The product saves time, reduces guesswork, and increases the probability of finding a valuable problem worth solving.
